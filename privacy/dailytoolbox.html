Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the DailyToolBox app (‚ÄúApp‚Äù) collects, uses, and shares information. We request certain device permissions only to provide the features you use (such as PDF tools, scanning, QR, and file creation). We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect directly identifiable personal information like your name, phone number, or email address inside the app.

1. Permissions We Request and Why

A. Camera and Flashlight (CAMERA, FLASHLIGHT)

Purpose: To scan QR/barcodes and to provide document scanning features (Document Scanner), and to optionally use the flashlight.

Processing: Camera frames can be processed on-device using Google ML Kit. Images are not sent to our servers unless you explicitly share/export files using other apps.

B. Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO)

Purpose: For sound-related tools (e.g., decibel meter) or audio features that require microphone input.

Processing: Audio input is processed in real time for measurement. We do not upload audio data to our servers. If you actively record and save audio (if available), it is stored locally on your device.

C. Storage / Files (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)

Purpose: To let you pick files (PDF/Word/Excel/images), create new documents (e.g., image-to-PDF), and save results to your device.

Scope: The App accesses files you select or generate. It does not silently scan unrelated files. You remain in control via system file pickers and share dialogs where available.

D. Internet / Network State (INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE)

Purpose: To show ads (Google AdMob), check network availability, and (if enabled) download any online content required by features.

E. Vibration (VIBRATE)

Purpose: To provide haptic feedback (e.g., successful scan, action confirmation) and to support notification vibration. This permission does not provide access to personal data.

F. Battery Information (no special permission required in most cases)

Purpose: If the App includes battery-related tools, it may read battery level/status using standard Android APIs. We do not collect or upload battery usage statistics from your device.
